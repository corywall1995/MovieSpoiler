Plot:

V for Vendetta is a movie about a post WWIII Great Britain where a facist government has taken control. This facist government, lead by Chancellor Sulter, starts doing Nazi-like experimentation on the people they deem unfit for society (aka Jews, Muslims, homosexuals, etc.) and eventually these experiments yeild the worlds most deadly virus that the government then uses on its own people. They were able to create a vaccine for the virus from the blood of one of the prisoners, the prisoner from cell 5 (roman numeral V). Though this prisoner was immune from the virus, once he was exposed to it he lost his memory, he could no longer remember who he was or where he was from, so he takes the name "V" (his cell number). One day, on November 5th, the lab where they were performing these experiments had an accident where the whole facility went up in flames, none of the prisoners survived... except the prisoner from cell 5. The prisoner now burned and scared from the accident wears a Guy Fawkes mask and a robe to hides these injuries and dedicates his life to getting revenge against the facist goverment, its leaders, and those in charge of he detention facility. He eventually gets his revenge and is successful in toppling the governemnt, and killing the people in charge of the detention facility where he was held, however in doing so V was killed. His body was loaded on board a train full of explosives bound for parliment.
